Application Tips

Perfect your job applications with expert guidance on crafting persuasive cover letters, following up, and demonstrating professionalism.

Crafting Persuasive Cover Letters

  • Tailor Each Letter: Address the specific role and company, not a generic template.
  • Show Value: Highlight achievements and skills that directly solve the employer’s needs.
  • Be Concise: Stick to one page with a clear introduction, body, and closing.
  • Professional Format: Use a clean layout, formal tone, and proofread carefully.

Following Up After Applying

  • Send a Follow-Up Email: 1–2 weeks after applying, politely check the status of your application.
  • Reinforce Interest: Reiterate your enthusiasm for the position without sounding pushy.
  • Be Professional: Keep it short, polite, and respectful of the recruiter’s time.

Demonstrating Professionalism

  • Attention to Detail: Ensure no typos, formatting errors, or missing documents.
  • Meet Deadlines: Submit applications before closing dates and follow instructions carefully.
  • Respect Communication: Respond to employer messages promptly and courteously.
