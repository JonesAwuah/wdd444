Interview Coaching

Learn how to ace your job interview and practice with real interview questions.

Interview Tips

  • Research the company and role before the interview.
  • Dress professionally and arrive early (or log in early for virtual interviews).
  • Practice common interview questions and prepare examples.
  • Show confidence: maintain eye contact, good posture, and smile.
  • Listen carefully, answer clearly, and don’t rush.
  • Ask smart questions at the end to show interest.
  • Follow up with a thank-you email after the interview.
