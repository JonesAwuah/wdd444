Career Growth Roadmap

  • Step 1: Entry-Level Focus on learning, gaining experience, and developing basic skills.
  • Step 2: Skill Building Take on projects, earn certifications, and strengthen technical and soft skills.
  • Step 3: Mid-Level Growth Start leading small teams, expand your responsibilities, and network actively.
  • Step 4: Leadership Path Pursue management or specialized expertise, mentor others, and align with industry trends.
  • Step 5: Senior/Executive Shape company strategy, influence decisions, and become an industry thought leader.
